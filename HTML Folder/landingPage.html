Online Document Request System

The BTECH E-Request Online Document Request System is an innovative platform designed to simplify and streamline the process of requesting official documents for both current students and alumni of Dalubhasaang Politekniko ng Lungsod ng Baliwag. This digital solution allows users to conveniently submit requests for various academic and administrative documents anytime and anywhere, eliminating the need for in-person visits and long waiting times.

Through the E-Request system, students and alumni can easily request important documents such as Transcript of Records (TOR), Certificates of Enrollment, Good Moral Certificates, Diploma Requests, Honorable Dismissal, and other academic-related certifications. Each request is securely processed and carefully reviewed by our dedicated administrative staff to ensure accuracy and efficiency.

The platform is user-friendly, secure, and designed to cater specifically to the needs of the BTECH community. Users simply need to fill out an online form, upload necessary supporting documents (if required), They will also be able to view real-time updates regarding the status of their request directly on the website, making the entire experience more transparent and convenient.

Our E-Request System aims to foster a more efficient academic environment by minimizing paperwork, speeding up processing times, and offering greater accessibility for both local and international alumni. Whether you're applying for a job, pursuing higher education, or needing official documents for personal purposes, the BTECH E-Request system is here to serve you.
